Our story — ہماری کہانی

One of South Punjab's most trusted kilns.

Started by Sufi Muhammad Aslam (Late) in 1983 and run today by his family with the same honesty and hard work. For more than forty years we have made strong, clean bricks for houses, shops and buildings all over Punjab.

The best local clay, a modern zigzag kiln — and one rule that never changed: every brick leaving the yard must deserve the name on it.
