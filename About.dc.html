The timeline

Fired, decade after decade

1983

The first fire

Sufi Muhammad Aslam (Late) lights the first kiln on Lahore Road, Hajian Wali — one yard, one promise.

1990s

A name in the district

Word travels the way it still does — mason to mason. Builders across Vehari start asking for our bricks by name.

2000s

Second generation

The founder's family takes the yard forward — keeping the ledger, and the standard, exactly as he kept them.

2010s

The zigzag conversion

The kiln is rebuilt around zigzag draft technology — hotter, cleaner firing and a Class-1 share the old kiln never reached.

Today

Punjab-wide

From Burewala to Lahore and Sialkot — our trucks deliver the same brick our neighbours built with in 1983.
