Our story — ہماری کہانی

One of South Punjab's most trusted kilns.

Started by Sufi Muhammad Aslam (Late) in 1983, Burewala Bricks is run today by his family with the same honesty and hard work. For more than forty years we have made strong, clean, high-quality bricks for houses, shops and buildings all over Punjab.

The best local clay. A modern zigzag kiln. And a simple rule that has never changed — every brick leaving the yard must deserve the name on it.

Technology

The zigzag difference

Most kilns push smoke and wasted heat straight into the sky. Ours forces air through a zigzag maze of brick chambers — burning hotter, cleaner and more evenly. Less coal in, more Class-1 bricks out, and cleaner air over the villages around us.
